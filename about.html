ABOUT ME

A curious creator

I'm a designer who loves creating thoughtful, user-focused experiences. With a background in product development, I'm all about solving problems and finding practical ways to bring technology and design together. I enjoy working in that sweet spot where creativity meets functionality, always making sure the user comes first.

I enjoy trying new things and picking up new skills. Recently, I started crocheting and made my first balaclava to tackle the windy Gothenburg weather. In my youth, I participated in numerous sports and my passion for sports runs deep. However, I feel like I missed out as a kid on dancing when everyone else was learning. So during the fall I took my first dance lessons! Luckily, beginner courses for adults exist, and it's never too late to try something new 🤠
