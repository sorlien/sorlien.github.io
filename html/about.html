About me

I see myself as a designer and a creative engineer. As a designer, I have a profound interest in human-centred design and user experience. With a background as an engineer I enjoy problem-solving and to find smart and feasible solutions.

In 2013 I was admitted to Design and Product Development, M.Sc. in Engineering, at Linköping University. The education is as much about design as solving problems and project management. I have both technical and design knowledge throughout the whole product development process, from idea to finished product.

In my youth, I participated in numerous sports and my passion for sports runs deep. To this day I still practice a wide range of sports and I love a good competition. Beside sports, I enjoy travelling. I like to explore new things, new places and won't say no to an adventure.
