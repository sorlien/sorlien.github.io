Introduction

Close to my home, there's a restaurant with affordable sushi. I eat there from time to time and their non-user-friendly website always surprises me. With a website, that's anything but pretty www.KaruizawaSushiBar.se/. I wanted to create something with a more appetizing look and feel. Starting with a new logotype, color theme and font selection, I ended up with a new visual profile.
