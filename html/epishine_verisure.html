Solar cell driven wireless sensor

2018 | MASTER THESIS

Background

In 2018, I finished my master thesis within the field of Product Development and Design Engineering. The thesis was conducted on behalf of Epishine in a collaboration with Verisure. The thesis followed a front-end product development process in combination with an iterative working method in order to find a solution in which an integrated solar cell reduces the amount of battery replacements in security sensors. The project covered everything from literature studies, benchmarking, tests, concept generation and validations.

Development Process

Two product development processes were combined in order to develop an innovative product. The method Double Diamond was used, due to continuous feedback from both Verisure and Epishine, resulting in an iterative process.
